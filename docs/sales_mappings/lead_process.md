# Lead Process Mapping

## AS-IS Steps (from AS-IS_Process.xlsx)
1. New lead received from Expo, Meeting, Call, or Referral
2. Enrichment of lead details
3. Virtual Contact or In-Person Meeting
4. Lead Scoring and Qualification
5. Lead Conversion to Opportunity

## ERPNext Implementation Steps
1. Create Lead in CRM module with source (Expo, Referral, etc.)
2. Enrich lead with details (name, company, contact)
3. Log activities (calls, meetings) in Lead
4. Use custom scoring fields or scripts for qualification
5. Convert qualified Lead to Opportunity in CRM

## Pain Points (AS-IS)
- Manual GRN, No batch tracking

## ERPNext Solutions
- Centralized lead tracking with activity logging
- Automated conversion workflow
- Real-time lead scoring
