# Logistics Management (Import) Mapping

**Simple Explanation**: Manage import logistics. ERPNext custom tracking.

## AS-IS Steps (from AS-IS_Process.xlsx)
1. After receiving the invoice and packing list, fill the google form.
2. Attach the document in that form.
3. Coordinate with logistics to book space in vessel/flight.
4. Logistics share the DSR report for the tracking of shipment.

## ERPNext Implementation Steps
1. After receiving invoice and packing list, buyer fills import form.
2. Attaches documents to form and shares with logistics.
3. Logistics coordinates booking space in vessel/flight.
4. Logistics shares DSR report for shipment tracking.

## Pain Points (AS-IS)
- Manual tracking.

## ERPNext Solutions
- Integrated visibility.
