# Scrap Management Mapping

**Simple Explanation**: Manage rejected material scrap. ERPNext Stock Entries.

## AS-IS Steps (from AS-IS_Process.xlsx)
1. We get approval from the operation head on the rejection list.
2. Identify the Scrap vendor by Admin.
3. Scrap the material.

## ERPNext Implementation Steps
1. Get approval from operation head on rejection list.
2. Admin identifies scrap vendor.
3. Create scrap Stock Entry and record sale.

## Pain Points (AS-IS)
- Manual approval, contact.

## ERPNext Solutions
- Workflow approvals.
