# Supplier Rating Mapping

**Simple Explanation**: Rate suppliers on performance. ERPNext custom rating fields.

## AS-IS Steps (from AS-IS_Process.xlsx)
1. Track supplier's delivery, response, IQC report, response on quality related issue.
2. Give rating according these data.
3. Share performance with the supplier.

## ERPNext Implementation Steps
1. Track supplier delivery, IQC, rejection in Supplier record.
2. Calculate rating based on performance metrics.
3. Share performance report with supplier.

## Pain Points (AS-IS)
- Manual rating.

## ERPNext Solutions
- Automated scoring.
