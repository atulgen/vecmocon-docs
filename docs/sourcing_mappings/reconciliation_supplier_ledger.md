# Reconciliation of Supplier's Ledger Mapping

**Simple Explanation**: Reconcile supplier accounts. ERPNext AP reports.

## AS-IS Steps (from AS-IS_Process.xlsx)
1. Supplier share the ledger.
2. Reconcile with vecmocon Tally.
3. If any discrepancy found, solve the issue.

## ERPNext Implementation Steps
1. Supplier shares ledger via email.
2. Reconcile with ERPNext Accounts Payable.
3. Resolve discrepancies via journal entries.

## Pain Points (AS-IS)
- Manual reconciliation.

## ERPNext Solutions
- Automated matching.
