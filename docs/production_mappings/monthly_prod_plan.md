# Monthly Prod Plan Mapping

**Full Forms**:
- EMS = Electronic Manufacturing Services

**Simple Explanation**: Align production capacity with sales requirements. ERPNext Production Plans handle this.

## AS-IS Steps (from AS-IS_Process.xlsx)
1. Consolidate monthly req from sales.
2. Check available FG stock.
3. Plan production for balance qty.
4. Prepare day-wise plan for EMS.

## ERPNext Implementation Steps
1. Update Production Plan with sales data.
2. Check stock levels.
3. Schedule production orders.
4. Assign to EMS via subcontracting.

## Pain Points (AS-IS)
- Manual consolidation, email sharing.

## ERPNext Solutions
- Automated planning, integrated with sales/stock.
