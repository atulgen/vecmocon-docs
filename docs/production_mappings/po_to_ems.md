# PO to EMS Mapping

**Full Forms**:
- PO = Purchase Order
- EMS = Electronic Manufacturing Services

**Simple Explanation**: Create POs for EMS subcontracting. ERPNext Purchase Orders linked to production.

## AS-IS Steps (from AS-IS_Process.xlsx)
1. Raise PO for job work and fixtures.

## ERPNext Implementation Steps
1. Create Purchase Order for EMS services.
2. Link to Work Order.
3. Track delivery and invoicing.

## Pain Points (AS-IS)
- Manual subcontract POs.

## ERPNext Solutions
- Integrated subcontracting workflows.
