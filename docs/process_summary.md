# Current Process Summary - Vecmocon Technologies

## Company Overview
Vecmocon Technologies specializes in EV components: Battery Management Systems, Vehicle Intelligence Modules, smart chargers, motor controllers. They use Odoo, Tally, Salesforce, Google Workspace, and manual processes.

## Key Business Processes

### Logistics
- **Contact Creation**: Manual verification via KYC, GST auto-fetch in Odoo.
- **PI/PO Creation**: Sales initiates, operations verifies NDA, creates in Odoo.
- **Dispatch Processes**: Tax invoice, repair/return, replacement, returnable, job work â€“ manual with LOT tracking, E-way bills.
- **International Imports/Exports**: Manual forms, freight forwarding, customs clearance.
- **E-way Bills**: Manual entry on portal.
- **PO Data Maintenance**: Excel tracking for bulk dispatches.

### Sales
- **Lead to Opportunity**: Salesforce for leads, enrichment, conversion.
- **Quotation and Pricing**: Draft to approved, with discount rules.
- **Post-Sales**: Project management in Salesforce.

### Finance
- **Tax Invoicing**: Manual in Tally, GST compliance.
- **Payments**: Advice reconciliation, vendor payments via sheets.
- **Sales Returns**: Credit notes, inventory adjustment.
- **GST/TDS/PF Returns**: Manual filings.
- **MIS Preparation**: Monthly P&L, balance sheet.
- **Budgeting**: Variance analysis manually.

### Inventory
- **Material Receipt**: GRN in Odoo, QC for mechanical parts.
- **Kitting**: Manual BOM explosion, transfer to EMS.
- **Outbound**: Email-based requests.
- **Physical vs System Inventory**: Manual reconciliation.

### Production
- **Sales Plan**: Excel-based forecasting.
- **Material Planning**: BOM checks, shortage identification.
- **Manufacturing**: Outsourced to EMS, reconciliation.
- **Quality Checks**: Incoming QC, PDI (missing), scrap management.

### Purchasing
- **BOM Ordering**: Negotiation, RFQ to suppliers.
- **Supplier Management**: Onboarding, rating (manual).
- **Payment Requests**: Approval workflows.
- **Import Logistics**: Form-based requests.

### Quality
- **Incoming QC**: Manual in sheets, rejection tracking.
- **FG QC**: Functional testing, no formal FG inspection.
- **PDI**: Not implemented.

### R&D
- **Order Execution**: SOR to handover, repair/replacement.
- **Procurement**: Google sheets for requests.

## Pain Points
- Manual tracking (Excel, sheets).
- No centralized system for all processes.
- Delays in approvals, reconciliations.
- Limited traceability for subcontracting.
- Integration gaps (Salesforce, portals).
