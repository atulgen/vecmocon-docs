# Bank Statement and Reconciliations Mapping

**Full Forms**:
- None

**Simple Explanation**: Reconcile bank statements with books. ERPNext bank reconciliation tool.

## AS-IS Steps (from AS-IS_Process.xlsx)
1. Download the bank statement from the respective bank portal.
2. Record all transactions from the bank statement into Tally.
3. Perform bank reconciliation by matching the entries in Tally with the bank statement.

## ERPNext Implementation Steps
1. Import bank statement into ERPNext.
2. Match transactions with vouchers.
3. Reconcile and clear items.

## Pain Points (AS-IS)
- Number of transaction is high due to which maximum time is spent in this part.

## ERPNext Solutions
- Automated matching and reconciliation.
- Bulk processing.
