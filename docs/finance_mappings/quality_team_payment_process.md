# Quality Team Payment Process Mapping

**Full Forms**:
- None

**Simple Explanation**: Process payments for quality-related services.

## AS-IS Steps (from AS-IS_Process.xlsx)
1. Quality team identifies payment need (e.g., audit service, calibration, testing fees)
2. Collect invoices and supporting documents
3. Prepare payment request
4. Approval mail trail
5. Forward approved request to Finance
6. Finance processes payment
7. Confirmation payment

## ERPNext Implementation Steps
1. Quality submits request
2. Approval workflow
3. Finance creates Payment Entry
4. Process payment
