# Third Party Payments Mapping

**Full Forms**:
- None

**Simple Explanation**: Process payments to third parties like legal teams and consultants.

## AS-IS Steps (from AS-IS_Process.xlsx)
1. Request for payment submitted by requesting department with vendor details
2. Collection of all invoices and supporting documents
3. Validation of service delivery or goods receipt
4. Verification of invoice accuracy and contract terms
5. Approval via email trail
6. Payment processing by Finance
7. Payment confirmation communicated
8. Record keeping and audit trail maintained

## ERPNext Implementation Steps
1. Department submits payment request with documents
2. Finance validates and approves
3. Create Payment Entry
4. Process payment
5. Confirm and record
