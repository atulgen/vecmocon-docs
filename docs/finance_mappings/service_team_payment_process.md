# Service Team Payment Process Mapping

**Full Forms**:
- None

**Simple Explanation**: Process payments for services rendered by service team.

## AS-IS Steps (from AS-IS_Process.xlsx)
1. Service team identifies payment requirement (e.g., vendor invoice, contract renewal)
2. Collect invoices and supporting documents
3. Prepare and submit payment request
4. Initiate approval process via email or workflow with Service Head
5. Obtain approval
6. Forward approved payment request to Finance
7. Finance processes payment
8. Confirm payment completion

## ERPNext Implementation Steps
1. Service team submits request with documents
2. Approval via workflow
3. Finance creates Payment Entry
4. Process payment
5. Confirm

## Pain Points (AS-IS)
- Missing documents

## ERPNext Solutions
- Workflow approvals, document tracking
