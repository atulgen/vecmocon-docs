# Advance Payment for Sourcing & Procurement Mapping

**Full Forms**:
- PI = Proforma Invoice

**Simple Explanation**: Process advance payments for sourcing and procurement.

## AS-IS Steps (from AS-IS_Process.xlsx)
1. Sourcing team issues PI and PO shares it via Mail
2. Approval for Payment via Mail
3. Finance verifies documents
4. Advance payment processed via bank

## ERPNext Implementation Steps
1. Sourcing issues PO
2. Email approval
3. Finance creates Payment Entry
4. Process advance

## Pain Points (AS-IS)
- Timing delays, document mismatch

## ERPNext Solutions
- Integrated approvals
