# HR Payment Process Mapping

**Full Forms**:
- HR = Human Resources

**Simple Explanation**: Process payments for HR-related requirements.

## AS-IS Steps (from AS-IS_Process.xlsx)
1. HR identifies payment requirement (advance or normal)
2. Prepare payment request details
3. Initiate email trail to HR Head with all relevant details for approval
4. Obtain approval email from HR Head
5. Forward approved request to Finance for processing
6. Finance processes the payment
7. Confirm payment completion and confirmation

## ERPNext Implementation Steps
1. HR submits payment request
2. Approval workflow
3. Finance creates Payment Entry
4. Process payment
