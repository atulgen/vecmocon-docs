# Advance Payment to Vendor Mapping

**Full Forms**:
- PI = Proforma Invoice

**Simple Explanation**: Process advance payments to vendors for critical procurement.

## AS-IS Steps (from AS-IS_Process.xlsx)
1. Production team issues PI and PO shares it via email Trail
2. Production Head approves payment in email trail
3. Finance verifies documents
4. Advance payment processed via bank

## ERPNext Implementation Steps
1. Production issues PO
2. Approval workflow
3. Finance creates Payment Entry
4. Process advance payment

## Pain Points (AS-IS)
- Delays in approval, document mismatch

## ERPNext Solutions
- Integrated workflows
