# R&D Capitalization and Amortization Mapping

**Full Forms**:
- R&D = Research and Development
- CWIP = Capital Work in Progress

**Simple Explanation**: Capitalize and amortize R&D expenses. ERPNext Projects and Asset modules.

## AS-IS Steps (from AS-IS_Process.xlsx)
1. Identification of R&D expenses at entry level.
2. Recording transaction.
3. Transfer the R&D expenses into CWIP.

## ERPNext Implementation Steps
Note: Out of scope per sheet.
1. Track R&D expenses in Projects.
2. Capitalize qualifying expenses.
3. Set up amortization schedules.

## Pain Points (AS-IS)
- Project wise R&D expense bifurcation.

## ERPNext Solutions
- Project-based expense tracking.
- Automated capitalization.
